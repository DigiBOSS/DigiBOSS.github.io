Project overview

The main objective of the project is to implement a new generation of digital platform and toolbox solutions (DigiBOSS) to train, exercise and verify different power system operational states and conditions of uncertainty. DigiBOSS platform solutions will serve as an automated decision support toolbox in transmission organization at the control centre.

DigiBOSS toolbox solutions and its application will contribute to secure and reliable operation of the power system, providing monitoring, protection coordination, and optimization and automation functions to the operation of the interconnected elements. DigiBOSS toolbox will contain:

  • Novel integrated interface and innovative processing environment to handle interaction between different databases and possibility to execute complex systems in an applicable time;
  • Adaptive information filtering to guarantee Cyber-secure system operation and risks mitigation;
  • Open-Source and vendor independent environment for the potential to integrate specific solutions for TSOs.

The goal of the project is to provide a set of the toolboxes with automated decision support – next generation of the digital platform, with the following functionalities:

  • Data handling from different sources, that provides a cohesive and integrated view of the system being measured;
  • Solutions to increase Situational Awareness;
  • TSO-TSO/TSO-DSO information exchange and flexibility activation from distribution grid;
  • Cyber-Physical-Social systems and data interoperability.

An essential means of reaching the goal of DigiBOSS is the experimental validation and demonstration of the situational awareness solutions, including cyber-physical-social system interoperability and interface to achieve TSO’s acceptance and trust. The intended solutions of DigiBOSS include innovative system integrating, with high reliability, robustness and interpretability capabilities, fulfilling functional feature requirements, thus covering high priority activity: dynamic adaptation and configuration, self-repair capabilities, the resilience of complex systems.
